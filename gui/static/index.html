Transport

Sampling

Applied to the next takeover. The real-time engine samples with temperature + min-p.

How it works

Two real-time Aria models (aria_base, aria_jazz) run on MLX and continue your playing note-by-note with grammar-constrained decoding. Pick a model, choose a Core MIDI input/output, press Load & Start, play, and hand over with AI takeover. The server owns the low-latency MIDI path; a server-created virtual output lets Pianoteq or a DAW receive the stream.
